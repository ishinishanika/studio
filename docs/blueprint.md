# **App Name**: LifeFlow Connect

## Core Features:

- Donor Profile Management: Allows users to create, update, and manage their donor profile, including blood type, contact information, and last donation date.
- Donation Request Creation: Hospitals or clinics can post urgent or general requests for specific blood types or components.
- Find Donation Opportunities: Enables donors to search for nearby blood donation drives or specific requests based on their location and blood type.
- Donation History and Eligibility Tracking: Users can log their donations, view their donation history, and receive notifications when they are next eligible to donate.
- AI-Powered Eligibility Checker Tool: A tool that uses AI to analyze donor-provided health information and common medical guidelines to provide an initial, informational assessment of eligibility for blood donation.
- Notification System: Sends notifications to eligible donors about nearby donation opportunities or urgent blood needs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, reassuring blue (#24508E) that evokes trust and professionalism. (HSL: 220, 60%, 35%)
- Background color: A very light, crisp blue (#EBF1F8) to maintain a clean, calm, and approachable aesthetic. (HSL: 220, 20%, 95%)
- Accent color: A clear, vibrant sky blue (#4DBADD) to highlight key actions and information, ensuring good contrast and visibility. (HSL: 200, 70%, 60%)
- Headline and body text font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for its modern, clean lines combined with a friendly and highly readable character, suitable for a health and community-focused app.
- Utilize simple, line-based icons that are universally recognizable and promote clarity without adding visual clutter. Icons should be in the primary or accent color.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout for displaying donation requests and opportunities, ensuring content is easily digestible and navigation is intuitive.
- Implement subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions for feedback on user actions, such as form submissions or data loading, to enhance user experience.
